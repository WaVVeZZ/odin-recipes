Simple grilled cheese recipe

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter onto two slices of bread
  2. Drizzle olive oil on pan and turn on heat to med-high
  3. Toast one side of bread
  4. Put the cheese between the two slices of bread with the not toasted side facing out
  5. Finish toasting both sides
