Cheese pizza recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Salt
  • Garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Roll out dough to preferred size
  2. Put pizza sauce on dough
  3. Place cheese on top of sauce
  4. add a pinch of salt
  5. Mince garlic and add on to top
