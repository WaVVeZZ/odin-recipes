Grilled chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon
  • BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Pat dry chicken with paper-towels
  2. Squeeze lemon onto chicken, season with salt and pepper
  3. Drizzle olive oil onto large pan on med-high heat
  4. Place chicken on pan and cook for about 5 mins per side. (cooking may vary depending on thickness of meat)
  5. Turn heat to low and add bbq sauce to both side and cook until sauce thickens
